About EWVox Synths


A small project originated from the idea of ||||'s Business Administration course. It went through several rebrandings and brainstormings until becoming EWVox Synths.
EWVox Synths aims to become a multimedia business one day, featuring virtual vocalists, AND VTuber services alike, to connect VTubing community with Vocal Synth community through music, synth music.
As a small extra, EWVox also aims to tell stories through its characters, and users being free to add their own headcannons to them.
